How to use the camera

1. Auto mode

To turn on auto-mode -> Go to /auto-mode/on

To turn off auto-mode -> Go to /auto-mode/off

Once movement detected for 3 consecutive seconds, photo is taken and sent via Gmail.

2. Manual mode

When auto-mode is off, we can take photos manually. To do so, go to http://{{host_port}}/take-photo-now.

When auto-mode is on, we can NOT take photos manually.

How to check the log or the last photo

1. To check the log and the last photo taken (boring version)-> Go to /check-movement

2. To check the log and the last photo taken (amusing version) -> Go to /check-movement-2
