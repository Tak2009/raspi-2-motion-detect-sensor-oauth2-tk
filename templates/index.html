At the moment, auto-photo mode is: {{mode | upper}}


To turn on -> visit /auto-mode/on

Once movement detected for 3 consecutive seconds, photo is taken and sent via Gmail.

When auto-mode is on, we can NOT take photos manually.

To turn off -> visit /auto-mode/off

When auto-mode is off, we can take photos manually. To do that, go to http://0.0.0.0:5000/take-photo-now.


To check if the auto-mode is on or off, come back here after you pass the URL param = on/off
